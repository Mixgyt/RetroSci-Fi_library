Positions

Son utilidades que sirven para manejar un mejor posicionamiento de los elementos, son esenciales en la personalización.

Positions basicos

Estos son los positions basicos y eseciales.

HTML
  
<div class="position-static">...</div>
<div class="position-relative">...</div>
<div class="position-absolute">...</div>
<div class="position-fixed">...</div>
<div class="position-sticky">...</div>

Como mover elementos

Esto se hace por medio utilidades extra tales como top, start,bottom y end.

Las cuales pueden tener los valores 0,50,100 cada una de ellas. (siendo cada valor porcentual a la hora de ser asignado).

HTML
  
<div class="position-relative bg-light-subtle" style="width: 100%; height: 200px;">
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 end-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-50 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-50 end-50 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-0 start-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-0 end-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
</div>

Centrar elementos

Para poder centrar elementos se utiliza la clases translate.

Ejemplo con .translate-middle

HTML
  
<div class="position-relative bg-light-subtle" style="width: 100%; height: 200px;">
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-0 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-50 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-100 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-0 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-50 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-100 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-100 start-0 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-100 start-50 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-100 start-100 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px; height: 50px;"></div>
</div>

Ejemplo mezclando .translate-middle-x y .translate-middle-y para dejar dentro del container los elementos.

HTML
  
<div class="position-relative bg-light-subtle" style="width: 100%;height: 200px;">
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 start-50 translate-middle-x bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-0 end-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-0 translate-middle-y bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 start-50 translate-middle bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute top-50 end-0 translate-middle-y bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-0 start-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-0 start-50 translate-middle-x bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
    <div class="position-absolute bottom-0 end-0 bg-light rounded" style="width: 50px;height: 50px;"></div>
</div>
